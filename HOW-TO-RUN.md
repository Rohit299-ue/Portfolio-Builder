## üìã Step-by-Step Usage

### 1. Create Account
- Click "Get Started Free"
- Enter username, email, password
- Click "Sign Up"

### 2. Login
- Enter your username and password
- Click "Login"

### 3. Upload Profile Photos (2 Photos Required)
- In dashboard, find "Upload Profile Photos" section
- **Hero Photo** (left side):
  - Click "Choose File"
  - Select your photo for the top section
  - Click "Upload Hero Photo"
- **About Photo** (right side):
  - Click "Choose File"
  - Select your photo for the about section
  - Click "Upload About Photo"
- Both photos will appear in the preview!

### 4. Upload Resume (Recommended!)
- In dashboard, click "Choose File" in Resume section
- Select your resume (PDF, DOC, or DOCX)
- Click "Upload Resume"
- **Magic!** Skills, experience, and education are extracted automatically

### 5. Review and Edit
- Check the extracted information
- Add or edit any details
- Click "Save Profile"

### 6. Add Projects
- Scroll to Projects section
- Fill in project details
- Click "Add Project"

### 7. View Your Portfolio
- Click "View Portfolio" button
- Your beautiful portfolio opens in a new tab
- Share the URL with employers!

### 8. Download Your Portfolio
- Click "Download as ZIP" - Complete package
- Click "Download as PDF" - Professional document
- Click "Download as HTML" - Standalone file

## üîß Troubleshooting

### If the application doesn't start:
1. Make sure Java is installed
2. Close any other programs using port 8080
3. Try running ULTIMATE-FIX.bat again

### If browser shows "Can't connect":
1. Make sure you see "Started PortfolioApplication" in the black window
2. Wait a few more seconds
3. Refresh the browser page

### If you see errors:
1. Don't close the black window
2. Take a screenshot of the error
3. The application needs the black window to stay open
